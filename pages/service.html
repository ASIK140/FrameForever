OPERATION PROCESS:

Frame Forever is dedicated to providing exceptional photography services tailored to our clients' needs. Our operation process integrates effective communication channels, collaborative discussions, and postevent evaluations to ensure seamless planning and execution of every photography project. Here's an overview of our operation process

Initial Communication:

Creation of WhatsApp Group: Upon confirmation of the photography project, we create a dedicated WhatsApp group including the client, key stakeholders, and members involved in the event planning and execution.

Introduction and Planning: The group serves as a platform for introductions, sharing project details, discussing requirements, and finalizing the photography plan.

Ongoing Discussions:

Collaborative Decision-Making: All discussions related to the event, including scheduling, location scouting, shot lists, and any special requests, are conducted within the WhatsApp group

Real-Time Updates: Regular updates and notifications regarding progress, changes, or any unforeseen circumstances are shared promptly within the group to maintain transparency and ensure everyone is informed

Pre-Event Meetings:

Detailed Planning Sessions: In addition to virtual discussions on WhatsApp, we schedule prevent meetings, either in person or via video conferencing, to delve deeper into the photography plan and address any specific concerns or queries.

Finalizing Details: These meetings allow us to finalize logistics, review the shot list, discuss contingency plans, and ensure alignment among all stakeholders involved in the event.

Event Execution:

Seamless Coordination: On the day of the event, our photography team operates efficiently, following the finalized plan and adapting to any on-site changes or requirements as communicated via the WhatsApp group

Communication Channel: The WhatsApp group remains active during the event, serving as a direct communication channel for any last-minute adjustments, updates, or additional requests from the client or event organizers.

Post-Event:
  • Raw Photo Sharing and Selection:

    • Prompt Sharing: Immediately after the event, we share all raw photos captured during the shoot with the client via the WhatsApp group

    • . Client Review: Clients are encouraged to review the photos at their convenience and make selections directly within the group, fostering real-time communication and feedback
    • Editing and Album Design Discussions: Transparent Communication: Our editing team provides regular updates and previews of edited photos and album designs within the WhatsApp group, allowing clients to provide feedback and revisions as needed.
    • Collaborative Decision-Making: Discussions regarding editing styles, color preferences, and album layouts are conducted openly within the group, ensuring that the final products align with the client's vision
  • Client Approval and Finalization
    • Presentation: Once editing and album design are complete, we present the finalized versions to the client via the WhatsApp group, accompanied by detailed explanations and visual previews.
    • Iterative Feedback: Clients have the opportunity to request revisions or adjustments directly within the group, fostering a collaborative approach to achieving the desired outcome.
  • Printing and Delivery Coordination:
    • A. Order Confirmation: Upon receiving approval from the client, we initiate the printing process and coordinate delivery details within the WhatsApp group.
    • Status Updates: Regular updates regarding printing progress, shipping schedules, and delivery tracking information are shared in real-time, ensuring transparency and peace of mind for the client.
  • Video Editing and Song Selection:
    • Collaborative Playlist Creation: Clients are invited to share their preferred songs or music choices within the WhatsApp group, facilitating collaborative playlist creation for video editing projects
    • . Video Previews and Feedback: Our video editing team shares previews and drafts of the edited videos directly within the group, allowing clients to provide feedback and suggest revisions as needed.

    At Frame Forever, our WhatsApp group-assisted post-event workflow ensures that every aspect of our photography and video services is guided by transparent communication, collaborative decision-making, and client satisfaction. From photo selection to album design and video editing, we leverage the power of technology to deliver exceptional results tailored to our clients' preferences and expectations.
